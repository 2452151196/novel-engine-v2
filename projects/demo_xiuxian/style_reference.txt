Style Reference - Shuangwen (Power Fantasy)

Target prose characteristics:
- Physical descriptions only. No similes. No emotional labels.
- Tight dialogue. Characters speak in short bursts.
- Action beats expand into environmental consequences (cracked floors, displaced air).
- Protagonist acts; others react and explain.
- Power shown through effect on surroundings, not adjectives.
- End chapters on concrete action or unfinished dialogue.

Example tone:
"The rain hammered the tin. The sound was metallic and sharp."
NOT: "The rain fell like nails, piercing the silence."

Key constraints:
- Zero "like/as if" comparisons
- No "not A but B" structures
- No profession-specific metaphors in dialogue
- No named emotions (angry, terrified) — show heat of breath, stillness of body
- Dialogue moves plot; narration carries space and consequence
